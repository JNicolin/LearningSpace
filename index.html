How to Play

Players take turns placing their mark in an empty space.

How to Win
  1. Navigate to get three of your marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).
  2. The first player to do this wins.
  3. If all spaces are filled and no one wins, itâ€™s a tie.
Tips for Winning
  • First: Start in the center for more chances to win.
  • Block Opponent: If your opponent is about to win, block their row.
  • Look for Diagonals: Diagonals can be an easy way to win.
  • Corners Help: Corners open up more possibilities for victory.
